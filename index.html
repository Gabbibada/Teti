Soul score

The metric that no one else measures.

Every episode rendered through Teti receives a Soul Score — a proprietary measure of the cinematic quality of the listener experience.

It accounts for pacing, emotional register, sonic coherence, listener engagement prediction, and narrative structure. A number that tells you not just how many people listened — but how deeply they felt it.
